What is hand lettering?

Almost everyone tries to be environmentally conscious today, but Osuva is looking beyond today. Their products are created with a sustainable future and their customers in mind. Their goal is to provide a pathway for each one of their customers to begin thinking about their future in a way that is accessible and reliable. Their focus is on saving for tomorrow, financially and environmentally.

What was the objective?

Osuva is a female - lead electronic company. Osuva is bringing a fresh take to the electronics world. They aim to make products that are non-binary in the packaging and use. In a male dominate area of work, the OSUVA team brings new, innovative ideas to the table. Not only is OSUVA environmentally conscious, they are people conscious. Their aim is to be accessible and repeatable to all potential buyers.
