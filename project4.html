WHAT IS BAUHAUS?

It's a German art school operational from 1919 to 1933 that combined crafts and the fine arts, and was famous for the approach to design that it publicized and taught. The Bauhaus was founded by Walter Gropius in Weimar.

WHAT DID YOU ACCOMPLISH?

I set out to design a this book in a way that would be visually intriguing by the reader. I chose to use plants to provide examples of the given text. I placed a heavy emphasis on creating visual examples to support Reynolds claims.
